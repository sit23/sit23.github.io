About Me

I did my undergraduate MPhys degree at the University of Oxford before heading to do a PhD at the University of Cambridge with Michael McIntyre. I moved to the University of Exeter in 2015 as a postdoc with Prof. Geoff Vallis. I then became a lecturer in the Maths department at Exeter in April 2020. You can find more of the details in my CV.

My personal interests include playing the trumpet, playing the bass guitar, and caring for my 2 children. I also love exploring Devon and Cornwall.
